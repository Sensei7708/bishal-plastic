Welcome to Bishal Plastic

Based in Mangaljan, Murshidabad, West Bengal, Bishal Plastic has been dedicated to producing premium plastic household items. Our product range includes buckets, containers, cups, and more — all designed to meet the everyday needs of Indian households.

We take pride in our quality craftsmanship, competitive pricing, and timely delivery. Every product we make reflects our commitment to durability and customer satisfaction.
