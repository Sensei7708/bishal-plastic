About Us

Know more about Bishal Plastic and our journey

Our Story

Bishal Plastic is a trusted manufacturer of high-quality plastic household items based in Mangaljan, Murshidabad, West Bengal. We specialize in producing a wide range of durable plastic products including buckets, containers, cups, and kitchen essentials that are designed to meet the daily needs of households across India.

With a strong commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, we have built a reputation for delivering reliable products at competitive prices. Our manufacturing process combines modern techniques with rigorous quality checks to ensure every product that leaves our facility meets the highest standards.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, durable, and high-quality plastic household items that make everyday life easier for families. We strive to continuously improve our products and services while maintaining the trust and loyalty of our customers.
